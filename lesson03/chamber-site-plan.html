Eagle River Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Eagle River Chamber of Commerce - The name represents the city of Eagle River, Alaska, where the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The website serves as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener, and community champion. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

Primary Colors:

  • Dark Blue for headings and accents
  • Light Gray for background and paragraphs

Typography

  • Body Font: Georgia, serif
